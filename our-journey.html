Our Journey
From London to Ghana, and Beyond

Women of Purpose began its journey in 2014 in London, with our very first conference featuring renowned talk show host Nana Churcher as our Guest Speaker.

From those early days, Women of Purpose created meaningful spaces for women to be refreshed, equipped, and encouraged through prayer meetings, prayer walks, DIY workshops, healthy living sessions, marriage and relationship talks, and practical life-building gatherings.

One memorable workshop included a healthy living session led by a local nurse, who shared valuable advice on hydration, wellbeing, and taking care of the body as part of living a balanced and purposeful life.

The vision was given to Pastor Amelia to bring together women from all walks of life and share her experiences in a practical, honest, and transparent way.

Her heart has always been to empower women to become all that God has called them to be — growing in wisdom, developing practical skills, building confidence, and reconnecting deeply with God.

In 2022, the vision expanded internationally with the launch of the Ghana chapter at the University of Cape Coast (UCC). Since then, conferences, Paint and Praise sessions, self-esteem workshops, and mentoring sessions have been held.

After a period of sabbatical, Women of Purpose Global was relaunched in January 2026 with renewed strength, clarity, and passion.

Our story is still unfolding.

Pastor Amelia aspires to see Women of Purpose chapters established across the world — developing Kingdom women, doing Kingdom business, walking boldly in their full potential, and becoming all that God has created them to be.
